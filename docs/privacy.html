Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Overview

Zylo is a self-hosted platform. This means YOU own your data. When you host Zylo on your own server or machine, no data is sent to us (the developers). You are the controller and processor of your own information.

2. Data Collection

We do not track, collect, or sell your personal information. The official Zylo website (this landing page) may use standard analytics for performance monitoring, but the Zylo application itself is completely private when self-hosted.

3. AI Features

If you choose to use the Gemini AI integration, your chat queries are sent to Google's API as per your personal API key configuration. This interaction is subject to Google's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. Zylo acts only as a client interface.

4. Contact

For any questions regarding this policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
